Directly Collected Physical AI Data

Two small robot data-collection episodes used as checkable source material for the Physical AI data review portfolio reconstruction.

This page publishes EP_017 and EP_018 in LeRobot format: parquet data, LeRobot metadata, episode context, and three synchronized camera videos per episode.

The public copy redacts local machine paths and device-port strings from episode_context.json. The LeRobot data files, metadata files, and MP4 videos are preserved.

What is included

Each episode keeps the basic LeRobot layout: data/ parquet frame data, meta/ metadata, and videos/observation.images.* MP4 streams. The metadata records action and observation-state vectors, timestamp/frame indices, and three camera observations: wrist, top, and belly.

This is a small portfolio sample, not a benchmark dataset. Its purpose is to make the portfolio’s Physical AI data-collection claim checkable and to show the kind of source material that the Workshop-style review workflow is meant to operate on.
