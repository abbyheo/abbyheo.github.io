Areas I’m Exploring

I’m interested in how organizations turn fragmented operational data into decisions people can trust and act on.

The problems I keep coming back to are not only about analyzing data after the fact, but about designing the workflows, quality signals, and review paths that help people understand what needs attention, what is ready to use, and what should happen next.

Recently, I’ve been exploring this through Physical AI data operations: how robot data collection results can be reviewed, how failures can be traced to their source, how scarce expert review time can be protected, and how data becomes ready for training or deployment.
